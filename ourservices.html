University Placements with Guaranteed Seats

We are proud partners of the University of Perpetual Help System DALTA, a leading institution with a sprawling 4-hectare campus and state-of-the-art facilities designed to train medical students to become the best physicians. With access to up to 283 hospital beds and a recognized tertiary teaching hospital, our partnership ensures a smooth application process and guaranteed seats at this trusted university.

Residency and Specialization Opportunities

We also collaborate with NMC accredited hospitals in the Philippines to provide seats for postgraduate residency programs, giving you valuable specialization options and hands-on experience.

Comprehensive Study Materials and Academic Support

From day one, we provide you with e-books, detailed case discussions, updated lecture notes, past exam papers, and more—all free of cost, providing you with efficient, targeted learning resources that give you the edge to excel and succeed throughout medical school.

Personalized Mentorship from Experienced Doctors

What sets us apart from other agencies is our unique team of medical doctors from Nepal, India, and the Philippines who reside close to the university, making them easily accessible and approachable anytime. By bridging cultural barriers and fostering strong connections between students and their institutions, we provide personalized academic guidance and heartfelt support tailored to each student’s needs.

Safe and Comfortable Lodging with Meal Plans

We arrange secure and affordable student housing near your university, ensuring a safe and comfortable stay. If you’re not satisfied with your current housing or want to find a new place, we’ll assist you in locating accommodations that better suit your preferences and budget. We also provide meal plans featuring Indian and Nepali cuisine, so you don’t miss home-cooked meals.

U.S. Clinical Internships for Hands-On Experience

During your fourth year, we offer an optional clinical internship program in the USA, which can be accredited as your official fourth-year internship. Through our partnerships with reputable hospitals across the United States, we facilitate your placement to gain valuable hands-on experience in a real clinical setting.

Our team supports you through the application, documentation, housing, and travel arrangements to ensure a smooth and successful clinical experience that enhances your skills and strengthens your postgraduate residency application in the USA.

Dedicated Local Support Team

From airport pickup to daily concerns, our on-ground support team is here to ensure you feel supported and confident throughout your stay in the Philippines.

Student & Parent Portal for Real-Time Updates

We are proud to be the only agency offering a secure, advanced online portal that empowers both students and parents to stay fully informed and involved throughout the academic journey. Through this portal, you can access real-time updates on grades, attendance, tuition balances, billing schedules, and payment history.
